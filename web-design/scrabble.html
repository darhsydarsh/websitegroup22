Product Description

The Original Scrabble Word Game from Mattel Games is the world's most popular word game. Players build interlocking words crossword-style on the Scrabble playing board.

How to Play Scrabble
Scrabble players draw seven letter tiles from the bag and try to make the best word using any of their tiles. The object of the Original Scrabble Word Game is to get the highest score. Each letter's unique value determines the points a player receives for their word.

Rack Up Your Points!
When playing the Original Scrabble Word Game, be sure to make the most of your board. More points are awarded when a word uses the triple letter, double word or even triple word squares on the grid.
