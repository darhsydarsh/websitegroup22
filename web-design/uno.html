Product Description

Race to get rid of your hand in the Uno card game. Play cards by colour or number until you only have one card left – UNO!

Uno Card Deck
The deck includes 19 Uno cards of each colour (red, green, blue and yellow), as well as 8 Draw, 2 Reverse and 2 Skip cards in every colour. 4 Wild cards, 4 Wild Draw Four cards, 1 Swap Hands card and 3 customisable cards complete your deck.

Action Cards
Create game-changing moments to defeat your opponents. Use the Swap Hands card to change hands with any other opponent and write your own rules using the 3 customisable Wild cards. Customisable cards are erasable too – so no Uno game is the same.

How to Play Uno
If you can’t play a card on your turn, draw from the central pile. The player to get rid of all the cards in their hand scores points for any cards their opponents are left holding. The first player to reach 500 points wins the Uno game.
