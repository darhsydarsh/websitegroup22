Product Description

The Guess Who? game is a classic tabletop guessing game for kids.

Choose the red board or the blue, and pick a mystery Guess Who? character. Players take turns asking yes or no questions to try and name their opponent’s mystery character. Do they have a beard? Brown eyes? What about a hat?

Close the doors as you rule out characters. Once you think you know who’s on your opponent’s card, make your guess. But beware – if the guess is wrong, you lose the game!

Players can use the scorekeepers to challenge opponents to a series of games in which the first player to win 5 games is the Guess Who? game champion. Please note that styles may vary.
